Welcome to Footballers Top Trumps

In this game you will be dealt 15 cards with stats from Premier League Footballers from the "so-called" Top 6 teams.

All stats will be from the 2021/22 Season.

In this game you will have to pick which stat your current player has that you think will be greater than the computer.

First to collect all 30 cards wins.

Good Luck!
